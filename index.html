About Indiana City Tours

Indiana City Tours was founded in 2010 by Roger Freeman, a Hoosier and history enthusiast. Growing up in Indianapolis, Roger loved to learn about his home state. After spending many years working at the Indiana State Museum, Roger decided to branch off on his own. Encouraged to share his love and knowledge of the state, Mr. Freeman started Indiana City Tours. His vision was simple: to provide engaging, informative, and fun tours that showcase the rich heritage and hidden gems of Indiana's cities. Today, Indiana City Tours welcomes thousands of guests each year, helping them uncover the unique charm and fascinating stories of Indianaâ€™s vibrant communities.

Why visit Indiana?

While Indiana may be labeled as the Crossroads of America, you're sure to want to stop here. Indiana is filled with tons of history, culture and beautiful scenery. Each city has its own story to tell, and attractions to entertain you and your guests. Not to mention, there are lots of great local restaurants to choose from. Whether you're a history nerd, an outdoor adventurer, or a sports fanatic, you're sure to find something to do!
